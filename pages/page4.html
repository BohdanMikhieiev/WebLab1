Reg is a robotic being who appears to be a little boy, and the deuteragonist of Made in Abyss. His actual age and origins are unknown, because he cannot recall anything about his life before his encounter with Riko. Riko names him "Reg", after a dog that she used to own. Later, it is revealed that he also went by the name "Reg" prior to ever meeting Riko. He is a member of Riko's squad, Hello Abyss.


Having a seemingly artificial body, Reg might be the only being completely immune to the The Curse of the Abyss. Because of this, he is sometimes referred to as "The Treasure of the Deep." Due to design similarities, Gaburoon wondered if Reg and they might share the same creator; noting that Interference Units don't cross layers and them having no data regarding Interference Units of Reg's kind.
