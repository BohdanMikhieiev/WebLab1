Prushka is the adoptive daughter of the White Whistle Delver, Bondrewd. Not much is known about her early life, as she fell victim to The Curse of the Abyss, resulting in intense physical and psychological damage from which she had to begin life anew. Although she no longer has a human form, her soul carries on with the delving squad Hello Abyss, in the form of a White Whistle. Her birth name from before her life with Bondrewd remains unknown.


Prushka is a young girl with short white and green curly hair and glowing red eyes. Her attire appears to be somewhat influenced by the clothing of diving suits. She wears a green coat and gloves on top of a white shirt, as well as a big hat on her head she almost never removes. A portion of her right ear is missing, though it is obscured by her hair.


Prushka is a cheerful and amiable girl who loved her adoptive father very much. She is incredibly devoted to him, and would do anything to help him, holding him in a higher regard than anyone else. Having lived the majority of her life in the 5th Layer, she developed a strong curiosity for exploring and was fascinated by tales of others travels through the Abyss. She is adventurous and quite brave, going on her own adventures through the 5th Layer behind her father's back.
